Salmon Recipe

Description

This recipe contains a perfectly cooked salmon that will impress your family and your friends!

Ingredients

  • 8oz Salmon Fillet
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Dill
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the fillet with seasonings of salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan and add a pat of butter with cooking oil
  3. Add fillet to the pan and sear both sides while basting with butter
  4. Remove once outside is cooked and bake for 25 mins at 375F
  5. Remove from oven and let sit for 10 minutes
