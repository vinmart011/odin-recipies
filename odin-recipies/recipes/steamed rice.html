Steamed Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe will show how to steam great rice that will never be overcooked!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of White Rice
  • Water
  • Butter (if desired)

Steps

  1. Thoroughly wash rice under cold water
  2. Add rice to rice cooker with water filled about an inch above rice
  3. Let cook for about 20 minutes
  4. Remove cover and fluff rice with fork
  5. Add butter if desired
