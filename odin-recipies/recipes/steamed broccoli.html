Steamed Broccoli Recipe

Description

This will show how to make wonderful steamed broccoli that will taste amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 2 Slices of Bacon
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the head of broccoli into florets
  2. Place a steamer into a saucepan and cover with some water
  3. Once boiling, add broccoli and cook for 3-5 minutes
  4. Add bacon to a cold pan and cook on medium heat
  5. Chop bacon into small bits
  6. Toss bacon, broccoli, butter, salt, and pepper in a bowl
